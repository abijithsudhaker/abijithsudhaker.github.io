Design of Smart System aided Bicycle
Aadithya Venkatanaryanan, Ashwin V, Adarshana Rajagopal  |  Spring 2017

We chose to address the problem of air pollution by designing a Smart Wi-Fi enabled air quality data collecting system attached to a bicycle. Air Quality values are pushed to the cloud and vizualized by the user on Google Maps once the ride is complete.

Report

IoT Analytics with Mobile Insight
Aadithya Venkatanarayanan, Tyler Davis, Nrithya Theetharappan, Srividhya Balasubramanian  |  Fall 2017

Deployed data consuming applications and used AT commands on the AT&T IoT Starter kit to obtain LTE network information and analysed the variation of network parameters like RSSI, RSRQ, RSRP, loading time etc., Developed C++ and Python based parser tools to visualize the relationship between the network and location.

Defense against DoS in VANET
Aadithya Venkatanarayanan, Anoosha Sagar, Nrithya Theetharappan, Srividhya Balasubramanian, Tyler Davis  |  Fall 2017

We simulated Denial of Service in VANETS where the RSU floods the vehicles with messages denying equal channel access and deployed a fall back RSU to reduce the impact of flooding and used channel switching mechanisms in DSRC to re enable medium access for the cars to communicate using messages, successfully reducing the impact of DoS in VANET

Report  /  Video

Smart Bike Telematics
Aadithya Venkatanaryanan, Ashwin V, Adarshana Rajagopal  |  Fall 2016

Built a sensor-microcontroller ecosystem using PIC18f around a bike in order to sense the driving environment and created an alert system with buzzer in case of fuel limit, speed limit, obstacles and motion around the bike
